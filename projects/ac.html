AC Service Website — Professional Repair & Maintenance

Responsive service platform for AC and home appliance repair with gallery, contact system, and admin upload control.

Overview

This AC Service Website supports a local repair business that offers AC, fridge, washing machine, RO water system, and electrical maintenance. Customers can explore services, view real work through the gallery, and contact the team easily. The site also includes a secure admin panel for uploading images and videos.

Key Objectives

Modern Business Website

Create a clean, responsive platform for a local AC/home service company.

Customer Engagement

Allow visitors to learn services and contact instantly.

Showcase Work

Enable the owner to upload and manage gallery content safely.
